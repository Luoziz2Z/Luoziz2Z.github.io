INTRODUCTION

This project is originated from the over-packaging and exaggerated advertising of the avocado industry, which has changed popular perception of avocados from common fruits to healthy and even diet products. So I wonder how advertising over-package common fruits, I would like to make a satiric brand called 'Tomate' to advocate reproductive health and sexual harmony to change the world's perception toward tomatoes from fresh fruit to sex and fertility product, and reflect the unscientific marketing methods.

Avocado advertsment in 1980s invites a beautiful actress to hold an avocado while showing off her exquisite body, seductively asking: "Will this body lie to you?" Impling avocados can make you healthier, more beautiful and sexier. Also, it associated avocado with super bowl and aristocrat, which made the common fruit become a new health life style.

TOMATO PEST ANALYSIS

Inspired by avocado, I wonder if any common fruit could break public's ordinary perceptions and generate new marketing directions. Then, I Found tomatoes.
