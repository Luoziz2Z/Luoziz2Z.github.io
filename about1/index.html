INTRODUCTION

I was amazed by the news that under-16 vocational school students had been forced to work as cheap labor in factories as a condition for their certificates in some places in China. Their situation reminds me of a kind of fungi called Matsutake. With people's perennial and immoderate picking, many of them were destroyed when they were still immature. I hope to use Matsutake's story as a metaphor for revealing the cruelty of child labor. Thus, I design a booklet showing the 'deprived childhood' and a stop-motion animation in the format of a “black fairy tale”, and exhibited them.

Avocado advertsment in 1980s invites a beautiful actress to hold an avocado while showing off her exquisite body, seductively asking: "Will this body lie to you?" Impling avocados can make you healthier, more beautiful and sexier. Also, it associated avocado with super bowl and aristocrat, which made the common fruit become a new health life style.
