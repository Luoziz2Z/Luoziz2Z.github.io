PEEK - 2020.11

This is a story about personal privacy, narrating the heroine Quan's childhood trauma caused by her mother secretly reading her diary. This experience led her to develop a fear of being followed by a shadowy figure. In a casual conversation with her roommate, she misunderstood their words as invading her privacy, triggering an emotional outburst again. Ultimately, surrounded by the gentle support of her roommates, she overcomes her inner demons and finally rescued herself.



PLEASE REPLY 2021 - 2021.6

The story tells the beauty of youth, with the heroin Luo as the first perspective, and the birthday as a metaphorical montage from ten years ago to ten years later, connecting the whole story. We invited Luo's high school classmates, and the original crew presented the most natural, pure and beautiful friendship between girls. We want to claim the joy of youth in everyone's heart through this video, let alone lament the passing of time, because there are always people who will surpass the limitations of time and space and accompany us forever.



GENERATION Z'S OUTLOOK ON RELATIONSHIPS - 2022.1

Young people in the Z era grow in an age of abundant materials. Most of them have been irrigated by Internet information in their youth, So they are neither too idealistic, nor to lose the expectation of love. So we did a two-week interview around the topic of Generation Z's outlook of marriage and relationships to figure out their various opinions on it.



WALTZ OF A WANDERING LITTLE PLANET - 2023.12

This is a story about my inner self. I remember the loneliest and most confusing times in my life when stayed alone in my room, and the balcony window became my only friend. Imagined myself floating in the air, like a lonely planet, I'm going through the same routine every day. While one afternoon, I heard children laughing from the nearby school. Their happiness brought me a simple but deep joy. So I transformed my experiences into a poem, hoping to inspire anyone feeling lonely to find their own source of little happiness in life.
